# Project ReUnion: Naming & Justification

## The Problem: The Transparency Gap
In the modern tech ecosystem, there exists a widening chasm between the accelerating pace of employer requirements and the static snapshots of employee skill stacks. This misalignment is fundamentally non-transparent. Employers often drift into "hiring scope creep," expecting unicorn candidates for standard roles, while job seekers remain unaware of how their current stack effectively depreciates against these silent market shifts.

## The Derivation: Re + Union
The name **ReUnion** is derived from a core philosophy of realignment:

*   **Re (Again):** Acknowledging that the employment contract is not a one-time transaction but a continuous cycle of re-evaluation and re-skilling. We are *re-visiting* the way skills are valued.
*   **Union (Together):** Bringing the disparate entities of "Job Requirement" and "Candidate Profile" back into a cohesive, transparent whole. It is a "union" of data points that have historically been siloed.

Thus, **ReUnion**: To bring together again that which has drifted apart—talent and opportunity—through radical transparency.

## The Metaphor: La Réunion
There is also a symbolic nod to **Réunion**, the French overseas department in the Indian Ocean. Just as the island is a geological hotspot born from volcanic activity—constantly reshaping its own landscape—our project addresses the volatile, shifting landscape of the job market. We aim to be the stable ground (the island) in the middle of the chaotic ocean of recruitment data. 

(Also, like the island, we hope this project is a beautiful destination for everyone involved.)


项目：ReUnion（重聚）——命名与理念阐释
问题核心：透明度鸿沟
在现代科技生态中，雇主需求的快速演进与雇员技能储备的静态快照之间，存在着日益扩大的鸿沟。这种错位本质上是缺乏透明度的。雇主常常陷入“招聘需求蔓延”的陷阱——以寻找“全能型独角兽人才”的标准来招聘普通职位，而求职者则往往意识不到，面对市场无声的变迁，他们当前的技能组合正在实际贬值。

名称由来：Re + Union
ReUnion（重聚） 的名称源于一个核心理念——重新校准：

Re（再次、重新）： 承认雇佣关系并非一次性交易，而是一个持续不断的重新评估和技能重塑的循环。我们旨在 重新审视 技能价值的界定方式。
Union（联合、结合）： 将“职位要求”与“候选人画像”这两个长期分离的实体，重新整合为一个连贯、透明的整体。它是历史上一直被孤立的两个数据点之间的“结合”。
因此，ReUnion（重聚） 意味着：通过彻底的透明度，将已经疏离的两者——人才与机遇——重新汇聚在一起。

象征隐喻：留尼汪岛（La Réunion）
此命名也象征性地向印度洋上的法国海外省 留尼汪岛（Réunion） 致敬。正如这座岛屿是火山活动形成的地质热点——持续重塑着自身的地貌——我们的项目旨在应对变幻莫测、不断重塑的就业市场格局。我们希望成为招聘数据这片混乱海洋中坚实稳定的陆地（那座岛屿）。

（同时，就像这座岛屿一样，我们希望这个项目能成为所有参与者心之所向的“美丽目的地”。）
